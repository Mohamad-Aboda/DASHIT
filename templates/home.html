DashIt

DashIt is a reporting tool that enables you to track your website performance metrics like page views, conversion rate, bounce rate, and more.

  • Create your DASHIT account

    Enter your email address,username and create a password, that's it!

  • Upload your data

    Upload your data to DashIt, choose what kind of sales or pos or inventory dashboard that you want to create, pick your KPIs, and you're done! It's easy!

  • Create your new dashboard

    Start with our preset dashboard templates or create your own from scratch, either way, it's a matter of just a few clicks!
